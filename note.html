Description

This comprehensive study note covers FIR and IIR filter design fundamentals that are essential for the Digital Signal Processing final exam. The document includes detailed explanations of windowed methods, frequency sampling techniques, and step-by-step computations that will help you understand the core concepts.

Key topics covered include filter specifications, design trade-offs, practical implementation tips, and common pitfalls to avoid during exams. Each section includes worked examples and practice problems with solutions to reinforce your understanding.
