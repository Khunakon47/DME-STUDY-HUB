How to Get Started

It's quick and easy to contribute or find the resources you need. Whether you're a sharer or a learner, these simple steps will guide your journey.

Sign In or Register

Access your account to start uploading or exploring thousands of quality notes shared by DME seniors.

Upload Notes with Ease

Select your file, categorize it, and provide a short description. Our system supports PDF, DOC, and DOCX formats.

Wait for Approval

Each note is reviewed for quality and clarity to ensure it meets the needs of fellow students.

Engage with the Community

Give stars, leave comments, and follow other contributors to build connections and foster learning.
